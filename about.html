About Us

Welcome to The Rock of Salvation Church (TROSC) – a Bible-believing, spirit-filled church committed to raising kingdom-minded believers rooted in the word of God.

At TROSC, we believe in the power of salvation through Christ, the leading of the Holy Spirit, and building a strong foundation in faith – like standing on the Rock that never fails.

We are a family of worshippers passionate about prayer, teaching, service, and walking in the power and love of God.

Our Mission

  • Preach the Gospel of Christ to all nations
  • Raise strong disciples who walk in truth and power
  • Impact lives through spiritual and social outreach

Our Vision

To be a spiritual refuge where lives are saved, built, and launched into destiny.
